WASTE MANAGEMENT

Waste management is one of the fast growing segments of KVSRP trusted by the Central Government, various state governments, municipal corporations, and municipal councils across the nation. Our solid waste management services aim to contribute to the Swachh Bharat mission and foster a hygienic and eco-friendly environment. We began our solid waste management journey over a decade ago. Our first project within this service area was the door-to-door collection of solid waste for the Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC). Additionally, we took up various other tasks within solid waste management for our waste management clients. It included sanitary landfill site capping, Biomining, processing of municipal solid waste, etc., as per SWM rules and regulations. Over the years, we’ve amassed extensive experience and built unparalleled expertise across the solid waste management area. As a result, while serving GHMC and many other software companies for almost a decade, we were chosen as the solid waste management partners for various other municipal corporations and councils across India.

We also provide an efficient pick-up facility from the train station or bus stand. So don’t be worried if you are traveling alone or are new to the place. Do let us know the place and time of arrival, we will keep ourselves updated about any changes in the scheduled time and arrange for your safe arrival at our guest house.
