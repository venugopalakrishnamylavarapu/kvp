EMERGENCY

When an emergency strikes, you need someone who responds to you quickly and sends help in the shortest duration possible. At KVSRP India, we understand the criticality of emergency responses. Accordingly, we have developed emergency response teams that provide assistance, aid and emergency services across multiple situations.
Currently, our outreach includes Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Jammu and Kashmir, and Ladakh. However, we are rapidly covering other places in India. We cover two crucial areas under our emergency services – emergency police services and emergency medical services. Until now, we’ve assisted nearly eight million people with our emergency response services. In a way, we have contributed to minimizing damage across various forms.

The ESS also provides specialized emergency services through individual personnel and teams. These specialized capabilities may be found in one or more various disciplines, depending on the jurisdiction:

►Fire and Rescue Services.

►Emergency Medical Services.

►Emergency Management.

►Public Safety Answering Points (i.e., call centers)
