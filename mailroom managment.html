MAILROOM MANAGEMENT

KVSRP provides the complete Mail room solutions to its clients. Mailroom solutions provide a single point of entry for all incoming & outgoing documents like scanned paper, photo copies, fax, emails and other documents including mail recording and distribution to the concerned person / department.
The benefits with mailroom solutions are obvious. You attain increased efficiency and lower operational costs because all documents are distributed correctly. Mailroom solutions gives you control of your information flow since you get fast access to status reports and auditing trails. Smooth integration with back-end systems such as customer and database management.

We offer customized solutions to our customers through a self-managed model ranging from individual stand-alone to complex IFM services, delivering 360-degree assistance, powered by technology
