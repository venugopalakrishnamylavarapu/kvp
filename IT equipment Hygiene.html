DATA CENTRE CLEANING

The sensitive nature of data centres demands a specialist approach to cleaning and maintaining them. KVSRP employees are trained to pay the utmost attention to every little detail and use only suitable cleaning equipment, that keeps your data centre safe while reducing any downtime.

COMPUTER EQUIPMENT CLEANING

Our computer technicians perform a deep cleanse of the keyboard, monitor, mouse and CPU. These technicians use dust extractors, soft brushes, cleaning wipes and antibacterial solutions to remove all dust and debris from the interior and exterior of clients computers.

TELEPHONE CLEANING

Your telephone sets are the most used while still being cleaned the least. We provide deep cleaning and sanitation services for phone handsets, mobile phones, keypads, cords and even telephone lines. These services can be scheduled according to your wishes, giving you a clean, healthy and germ free environment to work in.
