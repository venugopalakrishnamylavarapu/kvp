Procurement Manager
  • Job Title: Procurement Manager
  • Experience Required: 06 to 08 Years
  • Job Summery: Looking for a candidate with minimum 6 years of experience for the position of Procurement Manager
  • Location: Hyderabad
  • Positions: 1
  • Salary package: Rs 4.0 to 5.0 LPA

Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Create and implement regional procurement strategies that are innovative, cost-effective, and incorporate the frowing complexities and challenges within industry.
  • Build long-term relationships with vendors in the industry.
  • Compare proposals for price and specifications
  • Negotiate with vendors to reduce costs.
  • Review contract specifications on behalf of the company.
  • Communicate with vendors to ensure that the product arrives in a timely fashion.
  • Build and maintain long-term relationships with critical suppliers.
  • Build Database and create a Vendor Bank for each Product.
  • Manage technological systems that track the shipment, inventory and supply of materials.
  • Lead transformation activities to build procurement organizational capabilities and improve procurement efficiency.
  • Prepare daily, weekly and monthly procurement reports.
  • Ensure adherence to all safety, health, and environmental rules and regulations.
  • Keep abreast of changing industry trends.
  • Liaises with key company employees to determine their product and service needs.
  • Monitors business trends and product avalibility to pay the best price for company goods and services without sacrificing quality or delivery times.
  • Nurtures relationships with suppliers to negotiate the best prices for company.
  • Identifies and researches potential new suppliers.
  • Researches new products and services to meet company's goals.
  • Assesses total costs of company purchases.
  • Develops and implements strategies for procuring, storing, and distributing goods or services and maintaining stock levels.
  • Finalize purchase details of orders and deliveries.
